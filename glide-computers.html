Glide computing algorithms

Roberto Chao

We’re going to talk about algorithms. The algorithms running into glide computers that proccess the data obtained from the different sensors to give you information about your climb/descent rate, glide ratio, wind speed and direction, thermal drift, best route, etc. All this information makes you fly safer and better.

This algorithms are behind all glide computers in the market. From glide computers based on specific purpose hardware to glide computer software running on general purpose hardware like tablets, phones, e-readers.

We think that a more in-depth understanding of the way these algorithms work will help you correctly use the information your glide computer gives to you.
